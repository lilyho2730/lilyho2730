About me

Welcome to my portofoilo!

Hello, my name is Lily, an exchange student of Northeastern University, currently taking the course ENGL2730.

I had a passion for writing since I was very little. I am always reading extensively and writing extensively so as to improve my writing skills. I have developed keen interests in various genres of literature, including fictions, dramas and proses.

I am creating a portfolio for the course ENGL2730, and I would like to share with you what I have got so far.

I am sharing with you three pieces of my work. The first piece of work is about my fascinating first taste of surfing. The second piece of work is about my research on street food in Hong Kong. The last piece of work is about Golden Retrievers, my favourite dog breed.

Let's get started!
